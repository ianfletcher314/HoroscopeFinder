About Us
We are a group of programmers who met through a Trilogy coding bootcamp at Washington University in St. Louis. Although we have different skills and backgrounds, we all enjoyed the challenges posed by this project. Our team's task delegation allowed us to build the front and backend of our application simultaneously,  so we could come together at the end to focus on the finishing visual aspects.
